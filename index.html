Token Utility & Vision

Understand the dual purpose of HCST ‚Äì how it functions as a fundraising vehicle and aims to foster community participation, including a vision for potential profit sharing.

1. Fundraising

HCST aims to provide a transparent and efficient mechanism for raising capital. These funds will be dedicated to accelerating research, developing advanced robotic surgical techniques, expanding the Metaverse Hospital, funding innovative medical research, and creating educational platforms within the metaverse.

2. Community Participation & Potential Profit Sharing

The token is designed to offer holders an opportunity to become integral stakeholders in this futuristic healthcare vision. It is proposed that a portion of clearly defined profits, generated from specific activities related to Dr. Al Ansari's work funded by this initiative, will be distributed to HCST holders.

The precise mechanisms and definitions for profit sharing are currently under development and will be subject to rigorous legal and financial structuring. Distributions, when profits are realized and allocated, are anticipated to be made annually, potentially via stablecoins.
